Why BMW EV

Why people choose a BMW electric car.

BMW states that the high-voltage battery in fully electric vehicles is covered for up to 8 years or 100,000 miles from first registration, whichever comes first, alongside 3-year unlimited mileage electric vehicle cover [web:237][web:238][web:233].

That gives buyers confidence on both everyday ownership and long-term battery protection. If you are comparing petrol, hybrid, plug-in hybrid and full electric, this is where the real-life numbers start to matter.

Charging reality

Charging is getting easier.

The UK now has more than 118,000 public chargers, spread across more than 45,000 locations, and the network keeps growing [web:292][web:295][web:297].

By comparison, government figures say there are about 8,329 petrol stations in the UK, with an estimated 60,802 fuel pumps. The charging network is already large and expanding fast [web:300][web:296].

Questions

Quick EV answers.

How long is the BMW battery warranty?

BMW’s Electric Warranty includes three-year unlimited mileage vehicle cover and battery protection for up to 8 years or 100,000 miles from first registration, whichever comes first [web:237][web:238][web:233].

Do I need home charging to go electric?

Home charging makes life easier, but it is not the only way. Plenty of drivers mix home, work and public charging depending on their routine.

Is public charging still a pain?

It is much better than it used to be. The UK network is now huge, and rapid chargers are growing quickly, so motorway stops are less of a gamble than people think [web:292][web:295][web:299].

Will an EV suit my mileage?

That depends on your commute, how often you travel long distance, and whether you can charge at home. The calculator above is a good start, but Dan will go through it properly with you.

What if I am not ready to commit?

That is completely fine. This page is built to help you work it out first, not push you into a decision you are not comfortable with.

Can I part-exchange my current car?

Yes. That is usually the cleanest way to move into an EV, and Dan can help you see whether the numbers stack up.
