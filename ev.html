Why BMW EV

Why people choose a BMW electric car.

BMW states that the high-voltage battery in fully electric vehicles is covered for up to 8 years or 100,000 miles from first registration, whichever comes first, alongside 3-year unlimited mileage electric vehicle cover [web:237][web:238][web:233].
