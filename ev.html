Dan's story

I have always been a petrol head. I grew up around cars, I love the sound of a good engine and I was probably the last person you would have expected to go electric. So when I say this genuinely surprised me, take that seriously.

When I switched, the savings were noticeable straight away. No more fuel stops. Lower running costs. Servicing intervals that feel almost too good to be true. The car is smoother, quieter and faster off the line than anything I had driven before.

Since then I have helped many people make the same move, from committed petrol heads to nervous first-timers. Every single one has come back with the same reaction: "Why didn't I do this sooner?"
