Common questions

Things people usually ask Dan first.

How long is the BMW battery warranty?

BMW electric vehicles include three-year unlimited mileage vehicle cover, plus high-voltage battery protection for up to 8 years or 100,000 miles from first registration, whichever comes first.

Do I need home charging to make it work?

It helps, but plenty of people manage without it. If you have a driveway or garage, a home charger makes the whole thing easier. If not, Dan will help you work out a charging routine that fits your life.

Is public charging actually reliable now?

It is far better than it was. The UK now has over 118,000 public chargers, and rapid chargers on motorways are improving quickly. Most EV owners rarely rely on public charging for day-to-day driving.

What about longer journeys — will I make it?

That depends on your mileage and the model you choose. Dan will go through your actual routes with you, not a theoretical scenario. Most people are pleasantly surprised once they see the numbers.

I am not ready to commit yet — is that okay?

Completely fine. This is here to help you work it out at your own pace. Dan is not going to push you towards something that does not make sense for your situation.

Can I use my current car as a part exchange?

Yes, and that is often the cleanest way to move into an EV. Dan can give you a realistic idea of what your car is worth and whether the switch makes financial sense before you do anything.
